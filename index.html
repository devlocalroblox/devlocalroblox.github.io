Payment Terms

My core principle is that you never pay upfront. Payment can be handled in 3 different ways:

  1. Milestones (per approved task, pay after each core task is completed)
  2. Daily/weekly/monthly (pay every day/every week/every month)
  3. Upon completion (pay after all work is done; this payment method requires the most background checking & verification)

Note that a background check & proof of payment is required before development.

Percentage Deals (%)

Percentage deals are only accepted if:

  • A) Your game is already generating revenue (requires proof of revenue analytics)
  • B) Your game is legitimately expecting significant revenue (requires proof of ad funds & or an existing fanbase; also requires a personal interview). In some cases, you can hire me entirely on percentage long-term without fixed pay for brand new games if there is legitimate potential; I am always open to serious opportunities and willing to work with developers who are genuinely committed to the project's success.
  • C) The percentage is paired with a fixed payment. If game is not already generating revenue, or does not have concrete proof of expecting significant revenue, a percentage can be added to slightly reduce your fixed payment (though exact amount depends entirely on the revenue proof, project scope, and game potential.)

Percentages can range anywhere from 5% to 25%+
